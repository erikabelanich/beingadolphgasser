FILM FESTIVALS

DOCUTAH Festival 2026
February 26 - 28, 2026 | St. George, Utah
Founded at Utah Tech University by Emmy-winning filmmaker Phil Tuckett, presents new documentary films in the stunning natural setting of St. George, Utah.

Festival Information →

Being Adolph Gasser, has been selected by the Docutah International Film Festival, Feb. 26–28, 2026, in St. George, Utah. This was the first film festival I submitted it to and one of the best I have attended in the past. Come see the film in a great location with wonderful programmers.

In addition, one of my former students, Hisonni Johnson, had his Student Production Emmy Award–winning film, Legend Has It: The Remarkable Rise of Stephen McCain (created in my Documentary II class at CSN) selected as well. Teacher and student both showing films at the same festival! Now that is special...
