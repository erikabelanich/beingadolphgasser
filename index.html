Being Adolph Gasser

Adolph Gasser, a WWII veteran and renowned camera repairman, reflects on his 70-year career, including his friendship with Ansel Adams and contributions to the first Nikon camera. Despite modern challenges, filmmaker John C. Aliano captures Gasser's enduring legacy and the inspiration he provides to others.
